Gap Opening

How To Use The Site

History

When looking for trading methods put forth by many “professional” courses, one often finds that examples and instances of testing said trading methods are limited to some charts given by the course and find it hard to research these trading methods on their own. This website provides you the ability to test a trading method called “Gap opening”. If you are unfamiliar with this trading method feel free to read up more on it in the attached read me. The first chart displays the gaps (i.e. whether the market gapped up or down for the day), the second chart displays the resulting shift for the day (i.e. after opening did the market go up or down), and the third box displays the data for profits and how that individual stock did on its own (i.e. buying and holding).
